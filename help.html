Frequently Asked Questions

1. How to book a ticket?

Select route → Choose bus → Select seats → Fill details → Confirm booking.

2. Can I cancel my booking?

Currently cancellation option is not available.

3. Where can I see my bookings?

Go to "My Bookings" page from home.

Booking Steps
